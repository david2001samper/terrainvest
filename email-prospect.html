The Service

How It Works

A fully managed trading experience — you stay in control.

1
Your Account is Funded & Activated
Once your account on the Terra Invest VIP platform is funded to the required minimum threshold (USD $100,000 or equivalent), your account is verified and the service commences immediately. You'll have full access to the platform dashboard, live markets, and your portfolio.
2
I Send You Daily Trade Signals
Every Trading Day, your dedicated account manager will deliver your full set of trade signals directly through the platform and our designated communication channel. Each signal includes: asset, direction (Buy/Sell), entry price, stop-loss level, take-profit target, and position size — everything needed to execute confidently.
3
You Execute on the Platform
You retain full and unrestricted control over your account at all times. You execute each trade directly on the Terra Invest VIP platform with a few clicks. The platform shows your live positions, real-time P&L, and closes positions automatically at your targets.
4
Performance is Tracked & Reported
Your results are visible in real time on the platform — cumulative P&L charts, daily breakdowns, per-asset win rate, and full trade history. Your account manager will also send regular performance summaries and remain available throughout each Trading Day for any questions.
