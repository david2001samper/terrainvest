Article I — Definitions & Interpretation
1.1 Definitions
In this Agreement, unless the context otherwise requires: "Account" means the Client's trading account held within the Company's proprietary platform designated for the purposes of this Agreement; "Trade Signal" means any investment directive, entry/exit recommendation, or trade instruction provided by the Company to the Client via the platform or account manager; "Trading Day" means any business day during which the relevant markets are operational and the Company's management team is active; "Return" means the projected or historical performance metric communicated to the Client based on prior trading outcomes; "Dedicated Account Manager" means the named individual assigned by the Company to manage the Client's account relationship, provide trade signals, and handle all communications on behalf of the Company.
1.2 Interpretation
References to "Articles" and "Clauses" are to articles and clauses of this Agreement. Headings are inserted for convenience only and shall not affect the interpretation of this Agreement. Words importing the singular include the plural and vice versa.
Article II — Account Eligibility & Minimum Thresholds
2.1 Minimum Account Size
The minimum account size required to engage the Company's platform and managed investment services shall be One Hundred Thousand United States Dollars (USD $100,000.00) or its equivalent in any other freely convertible currency as determined by the prevailing exchange rate at the time of onboarding. Accounts falling below this threshold shall not be eligible for services under this Agreement.
2.2 Equivalent Currency Valuation
Where the Client's account is denominated in a currency other than USD, the Company shall determine equivalent value using the real-time mid-market exchange rate as published by authoritative financial data providers at the time of account verification. The Company reserves the right to request re-verification of account value if material currency fluctuation occurs.
2.3 Account Verification
Prior to the commencement of services, the Client shall provide verifiable proof of account funding meeting the minimum threshold. The Company may, at its sole discretion, employ third-party verification mechanisms to confirm the account balance held on the platform.

Article III — Trade Allocation & Daily Trade Limits
3.1 Trade Provision Framework
The Company's investment management division shall provide trade signals and investment directives to the Client on a per-trading-day basis via the platform. The number of trades provided is determined exclusively by the Client's verified account size, categorized into the following tiers:
Tier Account Size (USD Equivalent) Trades Per Day Classification
I $100,000 — $249,999 3 Trades Basic
II $250,000 — $749,999 5 Trades Standard
III $750,000 — $1,999,999 7 Trades Premium
IV $2,000,000 & Above Custom Elite
3.2 Nature of Trade Provision
All trades referenced in Article III, Section 3.1 shall be executed by the Client directly on the Terra Invest VIP trading platform. The Company's investment management team shall communicate the specific trade amount, entry point, stop-loss parameters, and take-profit targets for each trade signal issued via the platform interface or the Dedicated Account Manager. It is expressly understood and agreed that the Client retains full and unrestricted discretion to execute, modify, or decline any trade signal provided within the platform. The Company provides the venue for trading (the platform) and the advisory inputs, but the Client authorizes all final execution commands on the platform.
3.3 Non-Rollover Policy
Trades allocated for a given Trading Day shall not be carried over, accumulated, or rolled over into any subsequent Trading Day. If the Client fails to act upon, or the Company is unable to deliver, the allocated number of trades within the specified Trading Day, such trades shall be deemed forfeited for that day. This policy is strictly enforced to maintain disciplined risk management and ensure the integrity of the Company's trading methodology. No compensation, credit, or makeup trades shall be provided for unutilised daily allocations.
Article IV — Dedicated Account Manager
4.1 Assignment of Dedicated Account Manager
The Company shall assign to each Client a Dedicated Account Manager who shall be responsible for the day-to-day management of the Client's account relationship. The Dedicated Account Manager shall serve as the Client's sole and primary point of contact with the Company for the duration of this Agreement. All trade signals, performance communications, account updates, and service-related inquiries shall be channelled exclusively through the Client's designated Dedicated Account Manager.

4.3 Duties of the Dedicated Account Manager
The Dedicated Account Manager shall: (i) deliver all trade signals in real-time during Trading Days in accordance with the tier allocation set forth in Article III; (ii) provide the Client with the specified trade amount, entry price, stop-loss level, and take-profit target for each trade; (iii) communicate any material changes to market conditions, risk assessments, or trading strategies that may affect the Client's account; (iv) assist the Client with platform navigation and execution as needed; (v) respond to Client inquiries in a timely and professional manner; (vi) provide periodic performance summaries as reasonably requested by the Client.
4.4 Continuity & Replacement
In the event that the assigned Dedicated Account Manager becomes unavailable due to leave, reassignment, or any other reason, the Company shall designate a qualified replacement and shall notify the Client within twenty-four (24) hours of such change. The Company shall ensure a seamless transition with no disruption to the provision of trade signals or account management services. The Client may request a change of Dedicated Account Manager by submitting a written request to the Company's management, which shall be reviewed at the Company's sole discretion.
4.5 Exclusivity of Communication
The Client acknowledges and agrees that all trade signals, investment directives, and material account communications received from any source other than the Client's designated Dedicated Account Manager or the official Terra Invest VIP platform interface shall be considered unauthorised and shall not be relied upon. The Client shall not engage with or accept instructions from any other individual purporting to represent the Company unless such individual is formally introduced by the Dedicated Account Manager or confirmed in writing by the Company's management.

Article V — Historical Returns & Performance Indicators
5.1 Past Performance Disclosure
Based on the Company's historical trading records and audited performance data, the Company's managed investment strategies have, on average, generated returns in the range of approximately one percent to three percent (1% — 3%) per Trading Day. This figure is derived from the aggregate performance of all managed accounts on the platform over the preceding performance period and represents the mean daily return across varying market conditions and account tiers. Performance data shall be communicated to the Client by the Dedicated Account Manager on a regular basis.
5.2 Return Timeline
Returns referenced in Section 5.1 are calculated on a per-trading-day basis. The Client should expect the performance cycle to align with standard market trading sessions. Realised returns are reflected in the Client's account balance on the Terra Invest VIP platform upon the closure of each trade and are subject to the settlement timelines of the platform.

6.1 Account Maintenance
The Client shall at all times maintain the minimum account balance as stipulated in Article II within the Terra Invest VIP platform. Should the account balance fall below the minimum threshold due to trading losses or withdrawals, the Company reserves the right to suspend services until the balance is restored to the required level.

The Client shall maintain active and responsive communication with their Dedicated Account Manager, Sarah Lane, during Trading Days. The Client represents and warrants that their participation in this Agreement does not violate any laws, regulations, or regulatory directives applicable to their jurisdiction of residence or citizenship.
